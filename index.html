Frequently Asked Questions

How does NetScrape detect phishing websites?

NetScrape utilizes advanced algorithms and a constantly updated database to identify and block phishing websites in real-time. Our extension scans the URLs you visit and cross-references them with our extensive database of known malicious sites. If a match is found, NetScrape immediately alerts you and blocks the site to prevent any potential harm. Additionally, our machine learning technology helps detect new and evolving phishing threats, ensuring your browsing experience remains safe and secure.

What happens if NetScrape mistakenly blocks a safe website?

In the rare event that NetScrape mistakenly blocks a safe website, you can easily report the mistake directly through the extension. Simply click on the "Report Mistake" button in the alert popup, and our team will review the site to determine its safety. If the site is found to be safe, it will be added to our trusted sites database to prevent future blocks. Additionally, you have the option to visit the site anyway by clicking the "Visit Anyway" button, which temporarily bypasses the block for that session.

Can I customize the settings of NetScrape to suit my browsing habits?

Yes, NetScrape offers customizable settings to fit your unique browsing preferences. You can adjust alert levels, add trusted sites to a whitelist, and manage other security preferences through our user-friendly interface. This flexibility ensures you receive the protection you need without unnecessary disruptions.
